    'C.H.E.S.S is not effortless. Managing content, community, events, products, and personalized services requires significant time and attention.',
    'For individuals or small teams, the model can feel overwhelming if every component is launched at once. A better path is to start with one strong Content layer and one simple Hub.',
    'The model can also be complex to execute. Many teams struggle to connect all components or introduce monetization at the right time.',
    'C.H.E.S.S requires patience. Because it focuses on trust and relationships, results may take longer than direct-response campaigns.',
    'Another challenge is dependence on personal branding, especially at the Session level. If the founder becomes the only source of value, the model can become harder to scale.',
    'Finally, community management is difficult. Building a Hub is one thing; keeping it active, respectful, and valuable is another.',
