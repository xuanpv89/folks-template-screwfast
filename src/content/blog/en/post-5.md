    'C.H.E.S.S stands for Content, Hub, Event, Store, and Session. These five components guide the entire journey from discovery to deep engagement and monetization.',
    'Content is the entry point. It helps people discover you, understand what you stand for, and decide whether your work is worth their attention.',
    'Hub is the relationship center. It turns an audience into a community by giving people a more intentional space to ask questions, share ideas, and connect with each other.',
    'Event is the engagement catalyst. Livestreams, webinars, workshops, and meetups create moments of intensity where passive followers can become active participants.',
    'Store is the monetization layer. Products, services, courses, and memberships work best when they feel like a natural continuation of the value you have already provided.',
    'Session is the premium experience. Coaching, consulting, and small group mentoring create high-value interaction for the people who need a more personal path.',
